Details

The Church of the Firstborn in Yale is hosting a fundraiser event!

June 2nd 2018

Meal starts at 2pm & Concert starts at 5pm

We are calling it "A Firstborn Homecoming."

This is a wonderful opportunity for brethren from all over the country to fellowship, reminisce, and enjoy an evening of good gospel music!

RSVP ONLY

A meal will be served from 2-3:30pm. The concert starts at 5pm. Come Early to enjoy the meal and get a good seat!

Ticket prices: $20 for adults, $10 for children under 10. Children under 2 are free.

Live gospel music performances by:

  • Bro. Kerby Thompson, Sis. Marvel Brown, Bro. Landis Thompson, and Bro. Joe Chapman
  • Bro. Michael and Sis. Taylor Ude
  • The Heidlebaugh Family
  • The Tibbett Family
  • The Bryants (Sis. Hannah, Bro. Adam, and Bro. Eli!)

Meal prices: $15 for adults & $7.50 for children ages 3 through 10.

Meal includes:

  • Pulled pork sliders
  • Loaded mashed potatoes
  • Baked beans
  • Cinnamon rolls
  • Sweet tea

We will have items for sale at the event.

Items for Sale Include:

  • Event T-Shirt - $15 (XX or larger sizes $17)
  • Music CDs - $10 (Kerby Thompson & Joe Chapman or The Gospel 4)
  • Memories DVD - $20 (Audio and 8mm video segments of the brethren, group singings and stories of faith)

Proceeds Benefit the Yale Building Fund

Thank you for your generosity

PLEASE SHARE THIS EVENT

Everyone is encouraged to invite people from their friends list to join us for the event.

This is a church sponsored event, and proper conduct is expected. Everyone is welcome to attend, but the event is geared toward brethren in The Church of the Firstborn.

We hope to see you there! God bless!

If you have any questions, please call 918-617-6706.
