Details

Church of the Firstborn in Yale is hosting a “Gaither Homecoming” style concert event!

June 2nd 2018

2:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Details are still being determined, but here is a sneak peek of what to expect!

Live Gospel music performances including:

  • Bro Kerby Thompson, Sis Marvel Brown, and Bro Joe Chapman
  • Bro Michael and Sis Taylor Ude
  • The Heidlebaugh Family
  • And more!

Option for a professionally catered meal before the show

CD’s, DVD’s, T-Shirts, etc will also be available for sale

Many of the old brethren music cassettes will be made available for the first time to purchase on CD!

Proceeds benefit Yale building fund

We are preparing for a very large turnout, and have reserved space at the Payne County Expo Center in Stillwater. Tickets will be available to purchase early February. Although we have a large venue, we expect seating to sell out quickly!

This is a beautiful opportunity for Brethren all over the country to come fellowship and reminisce with us. Many details are still being ironed out, please follow this event and our Facebook page for future updates.

Please share this event!

Anyone is encouraged to invite people from their friends list to come join us for the event. This is a Church sponsored event, and proper conduct is expected. Anyone is welcome to attend, but the event is geared toward Brethren in The Church of the Firstborn.

Can’t wait to see everyone there! God bless!
